How it works

Online tutoring is possible thanks to an interactive online whiteboard that allows my students to see what I am writing (in addition to my face) in real time. I can put exercises there, the student can send me documents by taking pictures of them, and he can interact with the board. Lessons are personalised and generally individual (one to one). No software needs to be installed.

About me

Hello! I'm Authmane Terki, I live in Switzerland, more precisely in Lausanne. Freelance online tutor, ex developer, founder of Mathcam a Swiss company in online tutoring, I offer lessons in math, physics and chemistry, mainly for the levels "collège" and "lycée" of the French and Swiss education systems.

After several years of experience, I have been able to bring together many very valuable tips, techniques and resources, old exams and awesome exercises.

The satisfaction of the many students I have supported - many of whom have passed their exams - fuels my interest in teaching, while I have been passionate about math and physics since my younger age and qualified 3 times for the Swiss final of the International Mathematical and Logic Games Championship.
